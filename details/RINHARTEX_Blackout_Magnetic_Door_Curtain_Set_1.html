RINHARTEX Blackout Magnetic Door Curtain Set

Transform your space with our stylish and functional blackout magnetic door curtain set. Ideal for creating a private, dark, and comfortable environment in any room.

Key Features

  • 5-layer blackout fabric that blocks sunlight and UV rays effectively
  • Easy, no-drill installation with a magnetic closure and track system
  • Includes 1 curtain panel, 3 track parts, 12 hooks, 12 track rollers, and 1 adjustable tieback
  • Waterproof and thermal insulation properties for year-round comfort

Whether you're looking to enhance privacy in your bedroom, bathroom, or office, this curtain set provides a perfect solution.
