HSYLYM Teal Blue Spaghetti String Curtains

These HSYLYM Teal Blue Spaghetti String Curtains are a perfect mix of style and practicality. With a generous 280cm length, they can be easily customized to fit your doorway, window, or any opening perfectly. Their soft, opaque fabric ensures privacy while blocking excess light, making them ideal for both functional and aesthetic use.

The tassel fringe design adds a subtle bohemian charm, giving any space a warm and inviting feel.
