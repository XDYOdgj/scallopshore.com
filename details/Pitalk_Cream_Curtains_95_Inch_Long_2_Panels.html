Farmhouse Curtains with Geometric Patterned Design

Transform your living space with these Pitalk cream linen curtain panels. Featuring a soft blend of flax and polyester, they deliver both durability and a refined, flowing drape that blends effortlessly with rustic and modern interiors. Their unique geometric pattern adds a touch of bohemian flair, making your home both stylish and welcoming.

Versatile and Stylish

These geometric patterned curtains are designed to complement a variety of decor styles, from farmhouse and shabby chic to boho and minimalist. The 52x95 size is specially crafted for large windows and sliding or French doors, ensuring complete coverage and a seamless, cohesive look. Whether you're styling a living room or adding charm to your porch, these panels adapt to your needs with elegance and grace.

A Touch of Elegance and Rustic Charm

Ideal for patio doors, sliding doors, and French doors, they elevate the aesthetic of your home while maintaining practicality.
