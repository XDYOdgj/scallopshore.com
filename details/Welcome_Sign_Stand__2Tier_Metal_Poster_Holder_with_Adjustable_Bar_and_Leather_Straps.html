Introducing the LuvLuxe Welcome Sign Stand

The LuvLuxe Welcome Sign Stand is the ideal accessory to enhance the atmosphere of your special event. Whether you're planning a cozy baby shower, a romantic backyard wedding, or a festive party, this stand helps you create a professional and warm welcome for all your guests. Its two-tier construction allows you to display a greeting at the top and a venue map or seating chart at the bottom, making it easy to guide people with clear information.

The adjustable middle bar ensures flexibility, allowing you to fit different sign sizes without hassle. This makes it simple to update your welcome message or event details as needed. Designed with a strong metal frame, it's built to withstand the hustle of multiple events while maintaining its elegant and modern appearance. The stand includes four PU leather straps for secure and stylish hanging, adding both durability and a touch of sophistication to your setup.
