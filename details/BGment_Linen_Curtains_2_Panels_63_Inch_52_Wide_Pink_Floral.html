Elevate Your Space with Boho Farmhouse Linen Curtains

BGment brings a touch of boho elegance to your home with these beautifully crafted linen curtains. Combining the natural texture of linen with the durability of polyester, they deliver a soft, refined look that effortlessly fits into rustic, vintage, and farmhouse interiors. These curtains add a sense of warmth and character, making any room feel more inviting and stylish.

Featuring a hand-painted watercolor floral pattern in soft grey and navy, these BGment curtains are adorned with delicate leaf and branch motifs that enhance visual appeal and create a charming, artistic atmosphere. Each design is unique, ensuring your space stands out with a personalized touch while maintaining a cohesive, elegant feel.

Premium Quality and Timeless Design

The premium 20% linen and 80% polyester blend offers excellent softness and durability. This ensures your curtains maintain their shape and structure without wrinkling, making them a long-lasting addition to your decor.

Ideal for Light Filtering and Privacy

These light-filtering curtains allow natural light to gently enter your space while providing an elegant privacy solution. The medium room darkening feature helps create a calm, cozy environment during the day, perfect for bedrooms and living rooms.

Easy Installation and Perfect Fit

With a 63-inch length and 52-inch width, these curtains offer customizable coverage and a decorative drop that works seamlessly with your window size. They come equipped with 8 silver metal grommets for easy installation and a clean, modern finish.

Whether you're decorating a bedroom, living room, or farmhouse, these BGment curtains are the perfect choice to blend boho charm with modern functionality.
