Elevate Your Home with Stylish Farmhouse Curtains

VOILYBIRD's 2-panel farmhouse curtains are the perfect blend of bohemian charm and modern design. These curtains are crafted with a 40" width and 90" length, providing full window coverage with ease. The geometric tassel fringe adds a unique, eye-catching detail that enhances the boho vibe of your space.

Available in a vibrant sage green color, these curtains offer both style and comfort. Made from high-quality jacquard fabric, they are built to last, maintaining their color and texture even after multiple washes. Their thick construction ensures excellent insulation and privacy, making them an ideal choice for any room in your home.

With a simple rod pocket design and a 3-inch pocket, these curtains are easy to install and slide smoothly. Whether you prefer a neat and tidy look or a flowing, relaxed style, the versatility of these farmhouse curtains fits any lifestyle or aesthetic. They're the perfect addition for anyone looking to refresh their interior with minimal effort and maximum impact.

  • Easy installation with a single rod pocket and 3-inch depth
  • Thick material ensures privacy and enhances room insulation
