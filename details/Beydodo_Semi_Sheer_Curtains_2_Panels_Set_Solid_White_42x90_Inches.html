Beydodo Semi Sheer Curtains

Add a touch of elegance and refined beauty to your windows with Beydodo semi sheer curtains. These curtains blend style and functionality perfectly, offering subtle light filtering while maintaining your privacy. Their solid white color is versatile, fitting seamlessly into any room decor. Whether it's a bedroom, bathroom, or living room, these curtains enhance the ambiance with their soft, gentle appearance.

Each curtain measures 42" wide and 90" long, allowing for easy pairing to cover double windows or sliding doors. The pinch pleated design with hook ensures a secure and stylish window hang. Designed for everyday use, they are made from durable and easy-care polyester fabric, making them machine washable and tumble dry friendly without fading or shrinking.
