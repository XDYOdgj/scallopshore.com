Jinchan Linen Beige Grommet Top Curtain 100x108

Add a touch of elegance and charm to your home with this stylish and functional linen curtain. Perfect for large windows, it brings a perfect balance of light and privacy, enhancing any room's ambiance.

Stylish Design Meets Practicality

This soft, textured linen curtain combines rustic aesthetics with modern simplicity. The grommet top design ensures seamless installation on standard window rods, while the semi sheer fabric allows for gentle light filtering without compromising privacy.

Comfortable and Durable

Featuring a warm beige tone, this curtain is ideal for creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere in your living room, bedroom, or even patio sliding doors. The linen material is both lightweight and durable, making it perfect for everyday use and easy care.

Key Features
  • 100" x 108" extra wide linen curtain panel
  • Grommet top design for easy window rod installation
  • Lightweight and semi sheer for soft light filtering
  • Antique bronze finish adds farmhouse style appeal
  • Machine washable and durable for everyday use
How to Care for Your Curtain

To keep your curtain looking fresh and new, simply machine wash it in cold water with mild detergent and tumble dry on low. A light ironing or steaming session can help remove any minor wrinkles.
