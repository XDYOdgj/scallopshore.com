KGORGE 69-Inch Sidelight Curtain Panels

These sleek and functional sidelight door panels are engineered to deliver complete privacy and comfort. The 100% blackout fabric ensures that no natural light gets through, making them perfect for bedrooms, living rooms, or any space where light control is essential.

With thermal insulation built-in, these curtains help regulate room temperature by minimizing heat loss in winter and keeping cool air in during summer. This feature contributes to greater energy efficiency, helping you save on heating and cooling costs.

Installation is a breeze thanks to the included adhesive strips, which securely attach to any door frame without the need for drilling. Ideal for those who want a simple, modern solution for their windows and doors.

Crafted from a durable polyester blend, these panels are designed to maintain their shape and resist fading, ensuring long-lasting performance even with frequent use. Their neutral concrete grey color adds a contemporary aesthetic that complements most home interiors.

The adjustable strap gives you the flexibility to switch between a fully private look and a more open, decorative style when needed. Whether you're looking to block out the sun, enhance insulation, or simply add a touch of style, these curtains are a versatile and reliable choice.

Perfect for French doors, window openings, and spaces where privacy is key, KGORGE sidelight curtain panels offer a modern, practical solution for all your interior design needs.
